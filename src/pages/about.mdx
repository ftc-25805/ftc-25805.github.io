# About Team Reprogrammed

## Our Mission

We are Team 25805 'Reprogrammed,' representing Dayton Christian School with the mission to cultivate the next generation of Christ-centered engineers and innovators. Through competitive robotics, we develop technical excellence, creative problem-solving, and servant leadership that transforms both our team and community',


### FIRST Values
We embrace the core principles of FIRST - Gracious Professionalism, Coopetition, and making a positive impact in our community.
