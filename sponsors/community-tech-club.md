# Community Tech Club

Volunteer mentors and meeting space support from our local technology community. The Community Tech Club provides both space and expertise to help our team grow and succeed.

## Support Provided

- Meeting space for team activities
- Volunteer mentors with industry experience
- Access to additional tools and equipment
- Networking opportunities with local tech professionals

## Volunteer Impact

Their volunteer mentors bring real-world experience in software development, engineering, and project management. This mentorship helps our students understand how their robotics skills apply to future careers in technology.
